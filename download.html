Quickstart

No, seriously. Read this if you're new to Hideous Destructor.

  • Use slot 9 or look down in case you're bleeding to death.
  • Use medikits to stop bleeding. Fire to stop bleeding, altfire for longer-term healing.
  • Hit the reload key to reload. (Hold the key to avoid dropping the mag.)
  • Hit the unload key (user4) to strip ammo from your current weapon.
  • Hit the alternate reload and fire mode keys (user1 and user2) to operate certain alternative functions on your weapon. (Details in the manual.)
  • Tap or hold the use/open door key to pick up things and melee people.
  • Hit the user3 key to charge or reorganize magazines or batteries.
  • Hold the jump key in front of a low ledge to climb over it.
  • Hold the run/walk key to run.
  • Stop moving and hold the use/open door key to view all inventory.

Instructions

  1. Download, install, and configure GZDoom if you haven't already.
    Instructions for this may be found on the ZDoom Wiki. You must also have a legal Doom or FreeDoom IWAD. Hideous Destructor is designed for and recommends FreeDoom.

  2. Download Hideous Destructor from one of the links below.
    The downloaded file will usually be called source.zip or master.zip. While most Doom players will be familiar with WAD files (using the .wad extension), GZDoom can also directly load zip archives (sometimes using the .pk3 extension) or plain directories.

  3. Run the mod by drag-dropping the downloaded file onto GZDoom.
    Alternatively, you may use any of the alternate methods of running GZDoom, as detailed on the ZDoom Wiki.
